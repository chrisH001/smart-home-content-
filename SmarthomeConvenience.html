Smart Home's Convenience

Welcome to Smart Home's convenience Page!

Smart House's efficient self cleaning system includes a variety of advanced technology and eliminates any stress associated with cleaning or chores.

Smart Home's Self Preservation Systems:

Below are Smart Home's Self Cleaning Systems:

  • Robotic Mice: Smart House contains an abundance of robot mice, who will help you greatly by cleaning up dust and dirt off of every one of Smart House's surfaces.
  • Copper Scrap Mice: In addition to dust mice, Smart House also contains scrap mice, who clean up both small and large amounts of trash and deposit it in the house's incinerator.
  • Dish Washer: Smart House's abilities also extend to the kitchen, where it takes control of the dish washer and distrutes any dirty dishes you may have evenly, ensuring squeaky clean plates.

Smart Home's Convenience Technology:

  • Automated Voice System: Smart House contains an internal automated voice that reads off the time, date, and year. Smart House's voice system also provides reminders and schedules, ensuring you never forget your plans!
  • Complete House Control: Smart House's arguably most impressive feature is its ability to control chores and jobs throughout the entire house! Smart House provides breakfast, lunch, and dinner all to your convenience! Smart Home can also provide amenities based on your interests! For example, if you enjoy reading at a certain time, Smart Home will take this information and distribute you a book at your chosen time!
  • Additional Smart Home features: Smart House can also control internal temperature, water systems like baths and showers, the weather box, the patio layout, and the door operating systems!

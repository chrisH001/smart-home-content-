Smart Home's Safety Systems

Welcome to Smart Home's Safety Page!

This page is meant to display Smart Home's advanced anti intrusion and internal safety network.

Smart Home's Internal Safety Systems:

Below is Smart Home's fire defense and prevention system:

  • Fire Alarms: Smart Home contains sensitive smoke and fire detection systems that activate immediately at any signs of a fire, which then activates fire extermination systems.
  • Water Mice: In the case of a fire, Smart House will immediately send out robotic mice containing internal water supplies, which it uses to extinguish small scale fires.
  • Fire Extinguishing Tubes: These snake-like tubes are supplied with a special green extinguishing chemical, capable of eliminating even the strongest fires.

Smart Home's External Defense Systems:

Below is Smart Home's External Defense Systems Against Intruders:

  • Password Entry: The requirement of a password entry safely ensures that you and other Smart Home residents are the only ones able to enter Smart Home.
  • Voice Recognition: Smart House's voice recognition allows your beloved pets to safely enter Smart Home, while turning away unwanted visitors.
  • Automated Shudders: Smart House's shudders are useful in scaring any unwanted pests or visitors around it.
